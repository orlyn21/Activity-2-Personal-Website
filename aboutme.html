ABOUT ME

I am Orlyn Kate Losaria Engada, 19 years old, born on August 21, 2005 and I live in Brgy. Dabong, Janiuay, Iloilo. I love to write short stories and poems, read wattpad stories,watching movies, k-drama and anime.

My characteristics are shy, innocent, humble and kind. I am a quiet person, so I have this attitude that if you won’t approach or talk to me, automatically I won’t even dare to say a word or have a conversation with you.

My dreams are to graduate this course, save money, help my family and I want to enjoy myself to the things I’ve wanted, like travelling around the world to make moments into memories.
